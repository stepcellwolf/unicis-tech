About

Short Bio

Predrag TASEVSKI holds a Master of Science in Engineering in the field of Cyber Security from Estonia, where currently he obtained a degree Post-Master (Diplôme d'Ingénieur de specialisation) in Security in Computer Systems and Communications from Elite school EUREOM, France. His objective research interests are in the field of cyber security, cyber defence, security awareness, risk assessment, identity/risk management, cyber risk, cyber insurance, awareness-raising, socio-technical aspects, data scientist and hacktivist and so on. Predrag is the author of two paperback books: Messenger-Pigeon and Interactive Cyber Security Awareness Program, as well as being published in the magazines, blogger at ResPublica - academic blogging and also he is a Microsoft Certified Trainer and a Lead/External Auditor for ISMS. Today, he is an independent cyber security researcher.

Long Bio

I have finished my Post-Master (Diplôme d’Ingénieur de specialisation) in Security in Computer Systems and Communications at EURECOM.

As well as, visiting/remote lecturer in Faculty Humanistic Studies. I’m affiliated with Master of Cyber Security Studies at the University of Donja Gorica, Podgorica, Montenegro.

My Vita ->

Much of my research has been about cyber security as part of national security awareness, risk assessment, identity/risk management, cyber defence, cyber risk, cyber insurance, cyber crime, socio-technical aspects, methodological approach to awareness, password attacks, frequently occurring security incidents, threats, etc. My current work focuses on cyber risk and cyber insurance, defining correlation between threats vs. vulnerabilities, and likelihood of impact loss. I’m also interested in techniques and methods for data analysis and data science / mining.

Moreover, for everyday tasks usually I use: Emacs, org-mode, LaTeX, Linux (Lubuntu); windows 7, FreeMind, GIMP, and many other open source software. Additionally I use for development and for data mining the following: Python, R, Beautiful Soup, IPython and many others.

I am very interested in photographing and I own Nikon 80 DSLR, cooking, traveling and skiing. Sometimes, I go to yoga class, DJing - here is the music that I love and the one that I can not live without, and many other things.
