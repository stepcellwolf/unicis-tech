About

Short Bio

Predrag Tasevski MSc holds a Master of Science in Engineering in the field of Cyber Security, where currently is obtaining Post-Master in Communication and Security in France. His objective research interests are in the field of cyber security as part of national security awareness, risk assessment, identity/risk management, cyber risk, cyber-insurance, cyber security awareness and socio-technical aspects, hacktivist and so on. Predrag is the author of two paperback books: Messenger-Pigeon and Interactive Cyber Security Awareness Program, as well as being published in the magazines PenTest, Hackin9 and Nova Makedonija. He is also a Microsoft Certified Trainer and a Lead/External Auditor for ISMS. Today, he is an intern as Cyber Security Researcher based in Sophia Antipolis, France.

Long Bio

I study Post-Master (Diplôme d’Ingénieur de specialisation) in Security in Computer Systems and Communications at EURECOM.

I am Visiting/Remote Lecture in Faculty Humanistic Studies. I’m affiliated with Master of Cyber Security Studies at the University of Donja Gorica, Podgorica, Montenegro.

My Vita ->

Much of my research has been about cyber security as part of national security awareness, risk assessment, identity/risk management, cyber risk, cyber-insurance, cyber security awareness, socio-technical aspects and hacktivist. My current work focuses on the cyber risk and cyber insurance, defining correlation between threats vs. vulnerabilities, and likelihood of impact loss. I’m also interested in techniques and methods for data analysis and data science / mining.

Moreover, for everyday tasks usually I use: Emacs, org-mode, Linux (Lubuntu) & windows 7, FreeMind, GIMP, and many other open source software. Additionally I use for development and for data mining the following: Python, R, Beautiful Soup, IPython and many others.

I am very interested in photographing where I own Nikon 80 DSLR, and skying. Sometimes, I go to yoga class, DJing here is the music that I love and that I can not live without it, and so forth.
