Beer & Code #40: Drupal - winner in CMS security race

Date чет, 11 фев 2016 Tags drupal / cms / security / modules / audit / presentation / plugins / talk / beer

Feel free to look at my point of view of Drupal security approach. Here in the presentation over a beer I argue why Drupal for me is a winner when it comes in CMS (Content Management System) security. I have used WordPress for several years, however soon as I got introduced to Drupal from my earlier colleague and a good friend I started to love it. It took me a several week to get to know the way how Drupal works, and the modules = plugins, views, content, etc.
